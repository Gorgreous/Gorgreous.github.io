Company Introduction

​ Guang Xi Gorgeous enterprise is a cooker r & D and manufacturer in China, a national key high-tech enterprise and a listed company in the cooker industry. The company was founded on February 28, 2022. At the beginning of its establishment, the company took the lead in launching the new national standard cooking machine products, making Gorgeous brand cooking machine become the leader of the domestic cooking machine market. Since then, Gorgeous products deeply rooted in the hearts of people, Gorgeous has almost become a synonym for cooking machine products.
